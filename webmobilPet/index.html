Why Choose Toby's Pet Services

Our passion is to provide a loving, caring and safe grooming experience for your furry baby in a professional and stress-free environment. All pets will be treated with love and kindness, as well as from a professional and humane approach. It is our goal to assure owners that their pets will be treated with the utmost respect through their grooming journey. Let us pamper your furry baby in luxury like a rockstar.

Owners: Yessenia Pico and Toby

How we started...
It all goes back to the love for pets as a pet owner to multiple dogs and a cat who mean the world to me. Toby is one of my baby’s who has endured multiple surgeries for his herniated disk. Taking Toby to routine grooming appointments started to become challenging to say the least. Finding someone trustworthy, loving, patient and careful was a risk. Toby’s Pet Services became just an idea until one day the opportunity to assist thus gain experience allowed me to embark in this idea to take form into a company with team members who share the same love and compassion for furry babies.
